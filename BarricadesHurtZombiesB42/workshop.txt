description=A comprehensive Build 42 update that enhances zombie interactions with barricades, doors, windows, and now vehicles! This mod introduces sophisticated damage systems that make your fortifications truly matter in the zombie apocalypse.
description=
description=[h2]Key Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Fully functional vehicle damage system with intelligent part detection
description=[*]Material-based damage scaling for both barricades and vehicles
description=[*]Sophisticated angle-based vehicle part targeting
description=[*]Customizable sandbox settings for all damage values
description=[*]Visual feedback through blood effects
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]How It Works[/h2]
description=When zombies attack structures or vehicles, they take damage based on what they're attacking. The system intelligently identifies different materials and vehicle parts:
description=
description=[b]Material Damage Multipliers:[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Wood Structures: Base damage (1.0×)
description=[*]Metal Structures: 25% more damage (1.25×)
description=[*]Heavy Metal: 40% more damage (1.4×)
description=[*]Light Spikes: 50% more damage (1.5×) - Coming with SVU3 integration
description=[*]Heavy Spikes: 75% more damage (1.75×) - Coming with SVU3 integration
description=[*]Reinforced: Double damage (2.0×) - Coming with SVU3 integration
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Vehicle System[/h2]
description=The mod now features a sophisticated vehicle damage system that:
description=[list]
description=[*]Detects which part of the vehicle zombies are attacking (engine, doors, trunk)
description=[*]Uses customized detection ranges for different vehicle parts
description=[*]Implements angle-based targeting for realistic zombie attacks
description=[*]Applies appropriate damage multipliers based on material type
description=[/list]
description=
